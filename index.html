About Us

Rosticeria Da Gigi offers delicious dining, takeout and delivery to Hoboken, NJ.
Rosticeria Da Gigi is a cornerstone in the Hoboken community and has been recognized for its outstanding Seafood cuisine, excellent service and friendly staff.
Our Seafood restaurant is known for its modern interpretation of classic dishes and its insistence on only using high quality fresh ingredients.
